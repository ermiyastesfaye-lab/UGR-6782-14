Who am I?

Greetings! My name is Ermiyas Tesfaye, and I'm delighted to share a little bit about myself with you. Originally hailing from the vibrant city of Addis Ababa, I have embarked on a journey of exploration, growth, and learning in the field of software engineering. Education has played a pivotal role in shaping my path thus far. I began my academic journey at Embut School, where I completed my primary education. Later, I had the privilege of attending Vision Academy for my high school years, where my passion for drawing flourished. During this time, I was fortunate enough to showcase my artistic talent by winning drawing contests on two separate occasions, which served as a testament to my dedication and creativity. Fuelled by a deep interest in technology and its ever-evolving landscape, I enrolled in the Software Engineering Department at Addis Ababa University. Thank you for taking the time to get to know me a little better. I am excited to embark on this journey together and see where our paths may intersect.
